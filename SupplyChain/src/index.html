About Lost Sales Scenario

When a demand occurs and the item is out of stock, if the customer are willing to wait for the stock to be replenished, then the demand is recorded as backorder. Hence, it is a backorder when the customer will wait for the stock to be replenished whereby the demand is subsequently filled. Corresponding to this backorder there will be an associated penalty termed as backorder cost.
