About Backorder Scenario

In this scenario, when a demand occurs and the item is out of stock, the customers are willing to wait for the stock to be replenished. This demand will be recorded as backorder. Corresponding to backorder there will be an associated penalty termed as backorder cost. Backorder is to be immediately fullfilled once the stock is replenished.
