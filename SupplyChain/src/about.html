About Blockchain Enabled Supply Chain Role Play Game

Blockchain, the recently emerged distributed ledger technology has several attractive characteristics that favor the efficiency of a supply chain. Transparency is one of the core characteristics of blockchain. Information of transactions made in a blockchain network is accessible to all the peers (computer systems connected in the network). Information transparency has significant impact in supply chain management. It creates the opportunity for collaboration among the supply chain members. Supply chain collaboration can improve the performance of supply chains. Blockchain enabled supply chain role play game is a supply chain operation simulator that simulates a four stage serial supply chain that works under a blockchain environment. It is basically a team based game with four players in single team. Each team represents a supply chain that consists of retailer, wholesaler, distributor and factory. Each member has to take one role and take optimum order decisions to meet the customer demand. The ultimate goal of a team will be to minimize the total supply chain cost, which is the sum of inventory holding cost and shortage cost. In order to minimize the total supply chain cost, all the four players in a supply chain have to act collaboratively. Each role can make use of the blockchain ledger that contains the information of transactions made by other roles to take order decisions. Role play games can give insight on the dynamics of the business operations for the players. Blockchain enabled supply chain role play game provides the players an insight on how blockchain transparency influence the performance of a supply chain, through experiential learning. Blockchain enabled supply chain role play game is an extension of Supply Chain Role Play Game (SCRPG) developed in the Mechanical Engineering Department of National Institute of Technology Calicut.

ABOUT SUPPLY CHAIN ROLE PLAY GAME

This is a computerized and web based role-play supply chain simulation game in which participants enact a four-stage supply chain. This game lets the participants to experience typical coordination problems of (traditional) supply chains without information sharing and collaboration. Also, the package has the capability to share information among the supply chain members. The game can be used to provide participants with a more profound understanding of the reasons why collaboration technologies are used in contemporary supply chains to exchange information. The “Supply Chain Role Play Game Software Package” is developed in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, National Institute of Technology Calicut with the assistance of Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the institute. This software package has undergone several developmental phases and currently version 8 is available. First computerized web version was developed in the year 2010. This package has been used as for laboratory exercises for B.Tech (Production Engineering) and M. Tech (Industrial Engineering & Management) students of NITC since the initial year of its development. This game is also used for student/research scholar projects at Ph.D, M.Tech and B.Tech levels.
